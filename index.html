Ava Louise Untermyer

Ava Louise Untermyer is a MOVEMENT ARTIST. As a DANCER, Ava investigates ORGANIC movements to fluidly achieve an EXPANSIVE form of expression. As a CHOREOGRAPHER, Ava sculpts movements with INTELLIGENCE and precision to address themes of COMMUNITY and growth. As a GYROTONIC INSTRUCTOR, Ava invigorates PHYSICAL VITALITY within her clients. Through her work, Ava enlivens the ENERGY in motion.
