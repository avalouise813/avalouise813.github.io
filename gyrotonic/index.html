You Move: GYROTONIC® Expansion Method Classes

The GYROTONIC® Expansion Method balances and energizes the body to allow total freedom in movement. With the assistance of the GYROTONIC® Pulley Tower Equipment, these carefully crafted exercises both lengthen and strengthen the musculature. The sinuous spiraling movements enable the body to continuously open and expand to new extents. The GYROTONIC® Method is accessible to any-body, from professional dancers who want to elongate their lines to rehabilitating clients who want a low impact workout .

Benefits

  • Strong, lean musculature, with particular attention to the core
  • Enhanced flexibility & range of motion
  • Improved alignment, especially within the spine
  • Cardiovascular & aerobic stimulation
  • Greater circulation of blood, lymphatic fluids and energy
  • Increased coordination & integrated body use

Experience GYROTONIC® to unleash your vitality!

Ava's Gyrotonic® Background

Ava Louise Untermyer is a Gyrotonic® Instructor who moves to stimulate livelihood in others. While attending the Claremont Colleges, Ava participated in one of the few undergraduate Dance programs in country that offer a Movement Studies track. From this unique base, Ava brought easeful movement to a broad spectrum of individuals, working as a physical therapy assistant, beginning modern dance teacher, and creative movement instructor.

Ava's GYROTONIC® courses integrate this advanced understanding of movement. Her classes activate the deep supportive centers of the body while lengthening and releasing the superficial muscles. Ava caters to the specialized needs of each client with individualized exercises, hands on guidance and vivid imagery cues. Ava's GYROTONIC® courses allow her clients to experience the energy in motion.
